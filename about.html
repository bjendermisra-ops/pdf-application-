About ToolGi

We are on a mission to provide the fastest, safest, and most reliable digital utility tools to everyone, absolutely free of charge.

ToolGi was born out of a simple necessity: the internet needed a digital utility platform that didn't hide its best features behind a paywall, and didn't compromise user privacy by uploading sensitive documents to unknown servers.

Our Core Philosophy

100% Client-Side

We leverage modern web technologies to process all images and documents locally within your browser. Your data never touches our servers.

Forever Free

Digital tools should be accessible to all. We pledge to keep our suite of 50+ tools free to use, without limitations or premium barriers.

Who We Are

We are a passionate team of developers, designers, and digital advocates who believe in creating high-quality, enterprise-grade tools for everyday users. Whether you are a student resizing a signature for an exam portal, a professional compressing documents, or a creator formatting images for social media, ToolGi is built for you.
