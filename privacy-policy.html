Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to ToolGi. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website (regardless of where you visit it from) and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

2. The Data We Collect About You

Crucial Information on File Processing: ToolGi operates entirely on the client-side. This means that when you use our image resizer, compressor, or any other tool, your files are processed entirely within your web browser. Your images, PDFs, and documents are NEVER uploaded to our servers. We do not store, view, or share your files because they simply never leave your device.

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of non-personal data such as Technical Data (internet protocol (IP) address, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform) for analytics purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your data in the following circumstances:

  • Where it is necessary for our legitimate interests (or those of a third party) and your interests and fundamental rights do not override those interests.
  • To comply with a legal or regulatory obligation.
  • To improve our website, products/services, marketing, customer relationships, and experiences.

4. Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service and hold certain information. We also use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.
